Razerpay Merchant Services Malaysia Payment Acquirer

This module offers seamless integration of Razerpay Payment Integration with Odoo so you can accept online payments using Razerpay Merchant Services Malaysia on your Odoo website. This module offers customer convenience and faster payments.



From Razerpay Merchant Services Portal, Go to Transactions >> Settings to get the verify key and secret key



Configure verify key and secret key in Payment Acquirers



On the Payment page, Select Razerpay and click on Pay Now. You will be redirected to Razer Merchant Services payment page



Select the payment method from multiple payment methods



After a successful payment, you will be redirected back to the website's order confirmation page
